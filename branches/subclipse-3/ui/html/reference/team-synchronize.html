Team > Synchronize

When you are working with complex projects in a team, it may be extremely hard to grasp the impact of your changes and interaction with the changes committed by others. For this, Eclipse offers the Team Synchronizing Perspective, which Subclipse extends to support Subversion repositories. It can be switched to like any other perspective in Eclipse, or invoked directly by selecting 'Team > Synchronize...'

Note that invoking this action does not entail any changes being synchronized without your approval!
